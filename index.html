Assignment 2:

10/24/2022

For this assignment, I had repeated issues with controlling the size of my content when they were wrapped inside of divs or spans. It felt like with every section of the assignment I was having issues with content extending beyond its intended boundaries. To fix this, I had to carefully use the inspector to find where my boxes were being expanded and then use the max-width and max-height properties to correct those errors. I found that I didn't have problems controlling font sizes or colors but making sure that everything was wrapping in their own areas was the hardest.

In addition to controlling box sizes, I also had to learn how to appropriately control box positioning. To control the positioning of elements within the webpage I used flex boxes which took a lot of trial and error, as well as online help to get working. I was recommended the Flexbox Guide on css-tricks.com for help with flexboxes by one of the TAs and it was my most valuable resource for getting formatting and positioning for flexboxes correct on this assignment. I used many of the important features of flex boxes for this assignment and this webpage was extremely helpful for getting my code working. I also needed help with getting the correct syntax for other parts of the code, such as the box-shadow command. So, I used google for examples of these commands to implement them correctly in my assignment.
